## Day-by-day
- Day 1: Arrive in Tokyo, visit Meiji Shrine in the early morning, and explore the Shimokitazawa neighborhood for affordable food options.
- Day 2: Visit Tsukiji Outer Market for fresh sushi and seafood, then head to the Imperial Palace for a stroll around the beautiful grounds.
- Day 3: Travel to Kyoto by Bullet Train (approximately 2.5 hours), and check-in to a hotel in the Gion neighborhood. Explore the traditional temples and authentic food options in the area.
- Day 4: Hike the famous orange torii gate trail at Fushimi Inari Shrine in the early morning, then visit Kinkaku-ji Temple to admire the stunning golden pavilion.
- Day 5: Take a peaceful walk through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, and explore Nijo Castle to learn about its history and 'nightingale floors'.

## Where to stay
